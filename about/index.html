We are exe's Lounge;

A place where people relax and chat with each other to make new friends and journeys!


exe's Lounge was created as a server for just a group of friends. After a while, more people started joining and connecting with each other.
After that, we decided to close off the server for a while. We turned it into what the server is today; a community for everyone.
Not just friends, but for people from all over the world. To connect, to share stories, and to create new journeys.

8th of April 2020.
The server opened publicly. Everyone was now able to join and invite friends, no matter who it was.
That is the server we now know as our present exe's Lounge. A friendly community that welcomes every new member.
Our team continuously tries to find ways to evolve and grow the community.
